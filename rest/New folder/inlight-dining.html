Experience

The name, Vistara, is drawn from the Sanskrit word ‘vistaar’, which means limitless expanse. It reflects our belief that there’s no limit to anything we set out to achieve.

At Vistara, we firmly believe that the best part of the journey is not arriving at the destination, but the journey itself. We aim to push the boundaries of air travel and offer you a truly personalised and seamless flying experience that’s always delivered in an intuitively thoughtful way.

Onboard Experience

Vistara offers three distinct cabin classes to provide a personalised seamless service, which is thoughtfully delivered. The aircraft cabin has been thoughtfully designed to provide a conducive and pleasant environment for both business and leisure travellers. Regardless of which cabin class you choose to travel, we strive to ensure that you experience an enjoyable flight and arrive at your destination feeling relaxed.

Inflight Dining

At Vistara, we aim to provide an interesting and healthy culinary experience that offers a wide range of cuisines, which not only showcase the diversity of regional dishes, but also incorporates global culinary trends.

Business Class passengers will enjoy a special dining experience in the sky as our cabin crew meticulously carry out the meal service with fine linens and bone china tableware. Our Premium Economy and Economy passengers will be treated to wholesome and interesting food and beverage selections which delight and excite their palates. The menus have been carefully selected and will be rotated regularly to ensure variety for our frequent flyers.

We serve hot or cold meals depending on the departure time and duration of your flight. For flights of up to 40 minutes, cold refreshments are offered.

For special meal requirements, orders can be placed, through our Customer Service Centre or your travel agent up to 24 hours before the scheduled departure time. If you are purchasing your ticket on our website, special meal request can be made at the time of booking your flight.

We are delighted to provide Starbucks® coffee wherever Vistara’s customers are traveling. As two organizations who share a commitment to providing a distinctive and premium experience, Starbucks® and Vistara are a perfect pair.

Check-In Experience

We endeavour to make your journey hassle-free, starting with numerous options for check-in. However, regardless of how you check-in, please note that:

  • check-in will close 45 minutes prior to scheduled departure time.
  • customers are to be present at the boarding gate at least 25 minutes prior to scheduled departure time.
  • Make sure you are aware of your free checked baggage allowance. Any items over your allowance will be charged as excess baggage.
  • Don’t pack any valuables in your checked-in baggage.
  • Label your bags correctly with your name and address and remove tags and labels left over from previous flights.
  • Make sure you know your hand baggage allowance. and the latest restrictions on carriage of certain items in your hand baggage.
  • Make sure you take medication, important documents, valuable items and cash in your hand baggage.
  • Before checking-in please make sure you declare, if you are carrying any arms or explosive substances.
  • Battery cells in any electrical / electronic items are permissible now & will now not be removed at the security point. Loose battery cells / dry cells carried in the hand baggage are liable to be removed. Please carry such items in checked baggage only.
