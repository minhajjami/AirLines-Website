Preparing To Fly

At Vistara, your comfort and safety is our priority. You may be a seasoned traveller or flying for the first time but travelling by air entails a few preparations to ensure a pleasant experience.

You must ensure that you carry the necessary documents. These include tickets, valid photo IDs and other relevant IDs for concessionary fares you may be availing.

Check-in counters will open 2 hours 30 minutes before departure and close 45 minutes before scheduled departure time for all cabin classes. Give yourself enough time to reach the airport, keeping in mind traffic congestions, inclement weather and other delays.

Be certain that all your baggage has been tagged and identified. As some airports have more than one terminal, do ensure that you know which terminal you are departing from.

If you are nauseous due to in-flight turbulence, open the air vents, lie back with the seat in reclining position and relax. Air sickness bags are also provided in the seat pocket in front of you, should you require this.

You are advised to not experiment with self-medication. In case of early morning flights, avoid a late night with spicy food and alcoholic beverages the previous day.

Should you need any assistance onboard, please feel free to reach out to our cabin crew.
